Advocacy Efforts

Policy Research and Promotion:

This activity involves the examination and analysis of policies related to climate change, proposing specific measures. Additionally, the program focuses on promoting these policies through media channels and campaigns.

Technological Campaigns:

This initiative centers around encouraging the use of cutting-edge technologies to tackle climate change issues. It includes campaigns to raise awareness, build support, and facilitate the integration of technological innovations into sustainable practices.

Educational Web Programs:

This component aims to create and execute educational initiatives focused on climate change using web-based platforms. It leverages the internet to disseminate information, raise awareness, and provide resources for understanding and addressing climate-related issues.
